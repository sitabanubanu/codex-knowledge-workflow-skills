## Source

The source says a workflow should confirm first-hand material before analyzing video content. It identifies transcripts, subtitles, browser-visible transcripts, and transcribable local media as acceptable primary material.

## Inference

The workflow is designed to reduce false confidence by preventing metadata-only material from becoming a complete analysis.

## Extension

This pattern can be reused for research workflows where auditability matters more than producing a fast but unsupported summary.
